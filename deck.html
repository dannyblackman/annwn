Go to market

Sustainability consultants are the perfect channel to reach the whole market.

Consultancies are the perfect market entry partner.

  • Distribution multiplier: Each consultancy brings 50 to 100+ enterprise clients.
  • Data enrichment: Consultancy generates denser data, faster than any other channel.
  • Immediate economics: Consultants feel margin pressure most acutely.
  • Build trust quicker: Consultants have a lower adoption threshold than enterprise.

We grow through embedded experts, market reach and serving enterprise direct

  • Forward Deployed Sustainability Experts (FDSEs): embedded with early clients build the knowledge system from live engagements.
  • Scope / region: Over time the product breadth grows and we reach a global market.
  • Enterprise direct: When the consulting engagement ends, annwn helps the client retain the knowledge: creating a direct relationship and the foundation to expand into new regions and enterprise clients.
